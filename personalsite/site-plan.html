Overview

Purpose

Lines in the mall food courts often seem to be endless. Think about how convenient online orders have been for you in the past. Why don’t they offer that for malls? It would make everything so much easier! I create online ordering for food found within busy, crowded locations that we all love. MALLS. I collaborate with stores and restaurants in the malls to place all the food you love in one location. The same simple steps that we love outside of malls can now be found inside the walls of your shopping sanctuary.

Audience

We want to help the everyday person. The one who loves what we ALL do. FOOD and SHOPPING
