Payments We Accept

We Accept ALL!

Two options for payment. Online or in person at pick up. So credit/debit card information will be necessary if they were to order online, but also just cards or cash when they go to pick up in person. Here at Eat TheMALL we believe in simplicity and convenience. And because of that, we choose to accept all types of payments! Every card that is available, google pay, apple pay, and samsung pay are all supported within our app and website. We believe that by doing so, we don't leave anyone out of the experience of quicker food and faster pickups!
