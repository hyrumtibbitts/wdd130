Overview

Our target audience for the water rafting website is adventure-seeking individuals and groups who are passionate about outdoor activities and seeking thrilling experiences on the water. We aim to provide a comprehensive and exciting platform for individuals aged 18-45, who are interested in white-water rafting and related water sports.

Purpose text

Audience

What age group are we primarily targeting with our water rafting website? Our primary target age group for the water rafting website is individuals aged 18 to 45. This demographic includes young adults looking for exciting outdoor adventures and middle-aged individuals seeking to revitalize their sense of adventure. What are the interests of our target audience besides water rafting? Our target audience, besides their interest in water rafting, is likely to have a passion for outdoor activities such as hiking, camping, and kayaking. They may also enjoy adrenaline-pumping experiences, adventure travel, and exploring nature. What level of experience do we expect our target audience to have with water rafting? Our target audience may vary in their levels of experience with water rafting, but we anticipate catering to both beginners and intermediate rafters. We will offer a range of rafting experiences, from introductory trips suitable for novices to challenging routes for those with more experience.
