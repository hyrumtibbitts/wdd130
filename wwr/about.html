History

Adventure Rapids Rafting Co. was founded in 1985 by two friends who were inspired by a thrilling rafting trip. Over the years, the company grew, offering a variety of guided rafting trips, expanding its operations, and winning awards for its commitment to safety and sustainability. In the 2020s, they faced challenges like the COVID-19 pandemic but adapted and continued to thrive. Today, they offer a range of adventure experiences and remain dedicated to preserving the environment and providing unforgettable adventures.
